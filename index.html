Why America Needs Renewal?

America in the early 21st century faces a choice. The old liberalism of unchecked spending and bureaucracy has run its course. The conservative promise of tax cuts for the wealthy and neglect for the common good has also failed.

The New Democrats represent a new American center: pragmatic, responsible, and forward-looking. We believe in economic growth with fiscal discipline, social progress with personal responsibility, and national strength with international cooperation.

We are Democrats because we believe government has a role in empowering people. We are New Democrats because we believe that role must change for a new era.

Our Core Principles

Opportunity for All, Responsibility from All

Expand access to education and training so every American can adapt to a changing economy. Welfare-to-work programs that support those willing to strive, while ending dependency.

A Pro-Growth Economy That Works for Everyone

Balance the budget and reform taxes to stimulate growth. Encourage entrepreneurship through small-business tax relief and regulatory reform.

National Strength with International Cooperation

Invest in a strong military while fostering diplomatic relationships to tackle global challenges like climate change and terrorism.
