Our Vision for Renewal

The New Democrats represent a new American center: pragmatic, responsible, and forward-looking. We believe in economic growth with fiscal discipline, social progress with personal responsibility, and national strength with international cooperation.

Our Core Principles

A Pro-Growth Economy

  • Balance the budget and keep taxes low to fuel private investment.
  • Simplify the tax code and reward work, savings, and American innovation.
  • Support free and open markets while ensuring fair competition.

Modernizing Government

  • Cut waste and bureaucracy; government must be as efficient as the private sector.
  • Implement performance-based budgeting to measure results, not just spending.
  • Decentralize power and push decision-making closer to communities and states.

Opportunity & Responsibility

  • Expand access to education and training for all Americans.
  • Promote welfare-to-work programs that end dependency.
  • Encourage entrepreneurship with small-business tax relief.

Specific Policy Agenda

Education for a New Century

  • The Schools First Act: Tie federal aid to measurable results in literacy, numeracy, and graduation rates.
  • Teacher Excellence Incentives: Reward educators who deliver measurable student improvements.
  • Apprenticeship America: A national initiative offering tax credits to companies that sponsor apprenticeships, aiming for 500,000 new apprenticeships in five years.
  • Higher Education Affordability: Expand tax deductions for tuition and simplify loan consolidation to reduce student debt.

Infrastructure & Economy

  • Infrastructure Renewal Plan: Launch a ten-year, $300 billion program to repair highways and rebuild bridges.
  • Small Business Payroll Tax Holiday: Provide a two-year payroll tax holiday for new hires at small firms.
  • Community Bank Expansion: Expand federal loan guarantees for community banks that lend to small businesses in underserved areas.

Social & Fiscal Reform

  • Social Security and Medicare Protection Act: Reform these programs to ensure their long-term viability.
  • Balanced Budget Commitment: Enshrine a balanced budget amendment to the Constitution to prevent spending from outpacing growth.
  • Family Opportunity Credit: Expand earned income tax credits and provide a credit of up to $2,000 per child for working families.
