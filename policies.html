Our Core Principles for a New America

The New Democrats represent a new American center: pragmatic, responsible, and forward-looking. We believe in economic growth with fiscal discipline, social progress with personal responsibility, and national strength with international cooperation.

Opportunity for All, Responsibility from All

We will expand access to education and training, support welfare-to-work programs, and encourage entrepreneurship through small-business tax relief and regulatory reform.

A Pro-Growth Economy That Works for Everyone

Our goal is to balance the budget and keep taxes low to fuel private investment. We will simplify the tax code to reward work and savings, and support free and fair trade to open markets for American innovation.

Modernizing Government

Government must be as efficient as the private sector. We will cut waste and bureaucracy, introduce performance-based budgeting to measure results, and decentralize power to communities and states.

A New Social Compact

We will protect Social Security and Medicare through pragmatic reforms, encourage private savings, and support working families with childcare credits and family leave.

America's Role in the World

We believe in securing American interests without unnecessary interventionism. This means strengthening our military capabilities while keeping defense spending efficient and accountable, and ensuring trade agreements work for American workers and businesses.
